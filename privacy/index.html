Privacy Policy

Effective date: Jun 22, 2026

Overview

Windblock is a Google Calendar™ add-on that creates and updates calendar events based on public wind forecasts. This policy covers the Windblock add-on and the public website at windblock.app.

The add-on runs on Google Apps Script under your Google account. Windblock does not operate a separate user-account database or export the contents of your calendars to a Windblock server.

Google Data Access (Scopes & purposes)

Windblock uses these Google permissions for the reasons below:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.addons.execute — display and run the sidebar UI in Google Calendar™.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.locale — format dates/times and language appropriately.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.external_request — fetch public weather and ZIP geocoding data (e.g., Visual Crossing and Zippopotam).
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.storage — store your settings (location, wind threshold, hours, days ahead, paused) in Apps Script properties within your Google account.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar — find or create the dedicated calendar named “Windblock” and create, read, update, or remove its 1-hour forecast events.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.scriptapp — schedule an automatic refresh roughly every 4 hours (you can pause/resume).

Data the add-on accesses and stores

  • Calendar information: Windblock looks for a calendar named “Windblock” so it can reuse or create the dedicated calendar. It reads and manages events only in that calendar; it does not read event contents from your other calendars.
  • Your settings: location (ZIP code or latitude/longitude), wind threshold, daily hours, days ahead, paused status, dedicated calendar ID, and recent run status. These are stored in Apps Script properties associated with your Google account.
  • Forecast data: public weather and geocoding responses used to decide when Windblock events should be added, updated, or removed.

External services and data sharing

Windblock uses service providers only as needed to operate the add-on and website:

  • Google: hosts Apps Script, stores settings and operational logs, and provides Google Calendar APIs.
  • Visual Crossing: receives coordinates and forecast parameters needed to return hourly weather data.
  • Zippopotam: receives a ZIP code when one is entered so Windblock can resolve it to coordinates.
  • Google Analytics: measures use of parts of the public website, as described below.

No calendar event contents are sent to weather or geocoding providers. Windblock does not sell personal information or share it for advertising or cross-context behavioral advertising.

Website analytics

Parts of windblock.app use Google Analytics 4 to understand website traffic and whether visitors follow an Install link. Google Analytics may collect page views, session and engagement activity, approximate location, referrer information, browser and device information, and a pseudonymous client identifier stored in first-party cookies. Windblock also records an install_click event with the page path and the position of the clicked Install link. We do not send your Windblock settings or calendar data to Google Analytics.

You can limit this collection through your browser’s cookie controls or the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Operational logs

Google Cloud Logging receives diagnostic information from add-on executions, such as run and deployment identifiers, operation names, timing, response status, error messages, and hashed references to Calendar resources. These logs are used to troubleshoot reliability and authorization problems. Logging is designed to avoid raw locations, calendar IDs, event IDs, and calendar event contents. Google may attach its own pseudonymous execution or user identifiers to log entries.

Information you provide directly

If you email support or voluntarily use an external feedback or donation link, the information you submit is received by the applicable email, survey, or payment provider under that provider’s own privacy terms. Do not send calendar details or other sensitive information unless it is necessary for your support request.

Data we do not use

  • We do not use Google Calendar data for advertising, credit decisions, or unrelated profiling.
  • We do not allow humans to read your calendar event contents.
  • We do not include third-party analytics or advertising trackers in the add-on sidebar.

How your data is used

Your settings and public forecasts are used to create/update Windblock events. If a later forecast drops below your threshold, related events may be removed. An automatic refresh runs about every 4 hours; you can also click Refresh manually or Pause/Resume auto-refresh from the sidebar.

Retention & deletion

Your settings remain in Apps Script properties until you change them or use Windblock’s cleanup tools. The sidebar’s Uninstall… flow lets you disable the trigger, clear events, and/or delete the Windblock calendar, subject to Google API permissions. You can revoke Windblock’s access at any time from your Google Account permissions. Operational logs and website analytics are retained according to the applicable Google service settings and retention periods.

Security

Windblock runs on Google Apps Script, uses encrypted HTTPS connections to service providers, and uses Google Calendar™ APIs under your authorization. No method of electronic processing is completely secure, but Windblock limits data handling to what is needed to provide and troubleshoot the service.

Google API Services User Data Policy

Windblock’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements.

Children

Windblock is a general-audience productivity tool and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we’ll post the revised policy here and update the effective date above.
