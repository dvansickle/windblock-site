Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 23, 2025

Overview

Windblock is a Google Calendar add-on that creates and updates calendar events based on public weather forecast data. We respect your privacy and only request the minimum access necessary to function.

Data We Access

  • Google Calendar: Windblock creates, updates, and removes events in a dedicated calendar called “Windblock (ZIP)”.
  • User Properties: Your settings (ZIP code, wind threshold, hours, model, etc.) are stored inside your Google account using Google Apps Script UserProperties.
  • Weather Data: Forecasts are fetched from the public Open-Meteo API.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not export your calendar data to external servers.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
  • We do not use cookies or third-party trackers on the add-on.

How Your Data Is Used

Your settings are used solely to generate and maintain windy-hour events in your calendar. All processing runs within Google’s infrastructure under your account. If a forecast changes and winds fall below your threshold, previously created Windblock events may be updated or removed accordingly.

Security

Windblock runs on Google Apps Script and uses the Google Calendar API for secure access and storage. No servers operated by Windblock process or retain your data outside of Google’s environment.
