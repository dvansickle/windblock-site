Windblock Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 23, 2025

Overview

Windblock is a Google Calendar add-on that creates and updates calendar events based on public weather forecast data. We respect your privacy and only request the minimum access necessary to function.

Data We Access

  • Google Calendar: Windblock creates, updates, and removes events in a dedicated calendar called “Windblock (ZIP)”.
  • User Properties: We store your settings (ZIP code, wind threshold, hours, etc.) locally within your Google account using Google Apps Script’s UserProperties.
  • Weather Data: Forecasts are fetched from the Open-Meteo API.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect or store your calendar events outside of your Google account.
  • We do not share your personal data with third parties.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

How Your Data Is Used

Your settings are only used to generate appropriate weather alerts in your calendar. All processing runs inside Google’s servers under your own account.

Security

Windblock relies on Google’s Apps Script environment and Google Calendar API for secure access and storage. No external servers operated by Windblock process or retain your data.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at support@windblock.app.
