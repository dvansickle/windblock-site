Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 23, 2025

Overview

Windblock is a Google Calendar add-on that creates and updates calendar events based on public weather forecast data. We request only the access required to function, and processing runs within your Google account.

Google Data Access (Scopes & Purposes)

Windblock uses these Google permissions to operate. Each permission is limited to the stated purpose:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.addons.execute — runs the add-on UI inside Google Calendar (cards, buttons, forms).
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.locale — localizes date/time and language for the sidebar and event text.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.external_request — fetches public weather forecasts from Open-Meteo.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.storage — saves your settings (ZIP, wind threshold, hours, model) in Apps Script properties within your Google account.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar — manages calendars in your account, used specifically to create the dedicated “Windblock (ZIP)” calendar if needed.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events — creates, updates, and removes events in the dedicated “Windblock (ZIP)” calendar only.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.scriptapp — creates a time-based trigger that refreshes forecasts about every 4 hours.

Data We Access

  • Google Calendar: Windblock creates/updates one-hour events in a dedicated calendar named “Windblock (ZIP)”. Other calendars are not modified.
  • Settings stored in your account: ZIP code, wind threshold, hours, days ahead, and forecast model (via Apps Script properties).
  • Weather Data: Forecasts are retrieved from the public Open-Meteo API to decide when to add or remove holds.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not export your calendar contents to external servers.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
  • We do not read or modify your non-Windblock calendars.
  • We do not use third-party trackers in the add-on.

How Your Data Is Used

Your settings and public forecasts are used to create or update Windblock events in your calendar. When a forecast drops below your threshold, the corresponding holds may be removed so your calendar reflects current conditions. Automatic refresh runs via a time-based trigger roughly every 4 hours; you can also refresh manually from the sidebar.

Security

Windblock runs on Google Apps Script and uses Google Calendar APIs under your Google account. We do not operate external servers that store your calendar data; settings are stored within Apps Script properties associated with your account.

Uninstall

You can uninstall from the sidebar at any time. Options include disabling the trigger, clearing events, or deleting the Windblock calendar (subject to Google API limitations). You may also revoke access from your Google Account permissions.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will change the effective date above. Continued use of Windblock after changes means you accept the updated policy.
