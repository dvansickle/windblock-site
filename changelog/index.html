Changelog

Big improvements and fixes, listed by date. Newest first.

Oct 14, 2025
Provider UX Reliability
  • Switched to Visual Crossing for hourly wind — better coverage and stability.
  • Smarter Setup/Refresh: Setup rebuilds cleanly when you change settings or location; Refresh pulls the latest without wiping.
  • Clearer, shorter toasts that explain what was added now vs. what will arrive soon.
Oct 12, 2025
Cleanup UI
  • More reliable calendar cleanup during rebuilds.
  • Moved Uninstall… to the bottom of the sidebar and de-emphasized its styling.
Sep 15, 2025
Input
  • Added lat,lon input (e.g., 43.0730,-89.4528) alongside ZIP.
Aug 20, 2025
UX
  • New status messaging that shows how many wind hours were added right away.
Jul 5, 2025
Performance
  • Faster UI writes: adds the first few days immediately when there are many matches; the rest follow shortly.
Jun 10, 2025
Automation
  • Automatic background refresh every 4 hours to keep your calendar current.
May 1, 2025
Launch
  • Public beta: Wind alerts added directly to a dedicated calendar in Google Calendar™.
