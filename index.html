Questions

Before you install.

Is Windblock really free?

Yes. Windblock is completely free to install and use.

Which forecast does it use?

Windblock uses hourly Visual Crossing forecasts and checks for changes every four hours.

Can other people see the wind forecast?

No. People viewing your availability see the matching hours as busy. Windblock's event details stay in its dedicated calendar.

What happens when the forecast changes?

Windblock adds newly matching hours and removes holds that no longer meet your threshold.

Can I remove everything later?

Yes. The sidebar includes an uninstall flow that stops automatic refresh and removes Windblock's calendar data.
