1) Who we are

Windblock is a Google Calendar add-on that helps you reserve time when the wind looks good. It stores your settings in your Google account and uses third-party weather data to create/update events in a dedicated “Windblock (ZIP)” calendar.

2) Acceptance of terms

By installing or using Windblock, you agree to these Terms. If you do not agree, please uninstall the add-on and revoke its access in your Google Account settings.

3) Using Windblock

  • You provide preferences (ZIP, wind threshold, hours, days ahead, model) in the sidebar.
  • Windblock creates/updates events in a dedicated calendar. It also removes events when the forecast drops below your threshold.
  • An automatic refresh runs periodically (about every 4 hours). You can trigger an immediate refresh with “Run now.”
  • You can uninstall at any time from the sidebar; options include disabling the trigger, clearing events, or deleting the Windblock calendar (when permitted by Google’s APIs).
