How Windblock Works

Windblock adds hourly wind alerts to your Google Calendar based on your settings. This page shows how to install, configure, and verify it’s working.

Quick setup (5 steps)

  1. Install Windblock from the Google Workspace Marketplace and open Google Calendar.
  2. Open the Windblock sidebar. Enter your ZIP, wind threshold (mph), preferred hours (e.g., 6am–10pm), and days ahead.
  3. Select a forecast model (Auto is recommended). Auto prefers HRRR for short-range and falls back to GFS for longer horizons.
  4. Click Install / Update to save settings and enable automatic refresh (every 4 hours).
  5. Click Run now to create events immediately. You’ll see hourly events like “15 WNW ☀️” in a calendar named “Windblock (ZIP)”.

What the events show

Event title is “speed direction icon” (e.g., 15 WNW ☀️). Open an event to see temperature, precipitation %, wind direction degrees, the forecast model used, and the timezone.

Troubleshooting

  • No events? Click Run now. Verify your threshold isn’t too high and hours cover daytime.
  • Wrong hours? Check your Day start / Day end (exclusive) and that events use the forecast timezone.
  • Too many events? Lower Days ahead or raise your threshold.
  • Uninstall? Use Uninstall… in the sidebar (disable trigger, clear events, or delete the calendar).
